Our Services

FBS is Empowering Bronx Businesses and Advancing a More Equitable, Democratic, and Sustainable Economy. FBS is an online marketplace that connects Bronx institutions and nonprofit organizations with high-road, local businesses in order to meet their local purchasing goals and support a more sustainable, equitable, and democratic local economy.

Features

Connecting Buyers & Sellers

A sortable database of local businesses makes finding the right vendor simple

Providing Shared Business Services

E-commerce services, innovative financing tools, and customized online and offline support streamline transactions for both vendors and purchasers

Promoting Sustainable & Equitable Business Practices

High-performance and triple-bottom-line standards for vendors ensure that local purchasing benefits the broader Bronx community
